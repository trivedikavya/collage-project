# Negotio.ai — Autonomous AI Procurement & Negotiation

[cite_start]**Negotio.ai** is an autonomous platform that deploys specialized AI agents to negotiate, analyze, and optimize vendor contracts[cite: 1, 18]. [cite_start]Designed specifically for modern SMBs and SaaS-heavy startups, it acts as a **24/7 AI Procurement Officer** to ensure businesses never leave money on the table again[cite: 1, 2, 17, 27].

---

## 🚀 The Problem: Procurement is Broken
[cite_start]For Small and Medium Businesses (SMBs), vendor negotiations are often opaque, time-draining, and costly[cite: 9, 10]:
* [cite_start]**Financial Loss:** SMBs lose over **$10,000 annually** due to unoptimized vendor renewals[cite: 11, 12].
* [cite_start]**Time Inefficiency:** Manual negotiations average **15+ hours per contract**[cite: 13, 14].
* [cite_start]**Vendor Advantage:** A lack of market transparency gives vendors the upper hand during renewals[cite: 15, 16].

## ✨ Key Features

### 🤖 Autonomous Negotiation
[cite_start]AI agents communicate directly with vendor APIs and emails to negotiate terms and renewals in real time on your behalf[cite: 19, 20].

### 🔍 Contract Intelligence
[cite_start]The platform provides instant parsing and analysis of agreements to immediately surface hidden clauses, unfavorable terms, and auto-renewal risks[cite: 21, 22].

### ⚡ One-Click Optimization
[cite_start]Negotio.ai provides real-time pricing benchmarks and surfaces better vendor alternatives, allowing for data-driven procurement decisions[cite: 23, 24].

### 💰 Savings & ROI Tracking
* [cite_start]**Real-time ROI:** A dedicated dashboard tracks the measurable impact of every negotiation[cite: 64, 65].
* [cite_start]**Active Agent Monitoring:** Live status updates for all ongoing vendor negotiations[cite: 60, 61].
