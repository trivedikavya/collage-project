**Negotio.ai** is an autonomous platform that deploys specialized AI agents to negotiate, analyze, and optimize vendor contracts. Designed specifically for modern SMBs and SaaS-heavy startups, it acts as a **24/7 AI Procurement Officer** to ensure businesses never leave money on the table again.

---

## 🚀 The Problem: Procurement is Broken
For Small and Medium Businesses (SMBs), vendor negotiations are often opaque, time-draining, and costly:
* **Financial Loss:** SMBs lose over **$10,000 annually** due to unoptimized vendor renewals.
* **Time Inefficiency:** Manual negotiations average **15+ hours per contract**.
* **Vendor Advantage:** A lack of market transparency gives vendors the upper hand during renewals.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🤖 Autonomous Negotiation
 AI agents communicate directly with vendor APIs and emails to negotiate terms and renewals in real time on your behalf.

### 🔍 Contract Intelligence
The platform provides instant parsing and analysis of agreements to immediately surface hidden clauses, unfavorable terms, and auto-renewal risks.

### ⚡ One-Click Optimization
Negotio.ai provides real-time pricing benchmarks and surfaces better vendor alternatives, allowing for data-driven procurement decisions.

### 💰 Savings & ROI Tracking
* **Real-time ROI:** A dedicated dashboard tracks the measurable impact of every negotiation.
* **Active Agent Monitoring:** Live status updates for all ongoing vendor negotiations.

---

## 🛠️ Performance-Based Business Model
Negotio.ai utilizes a **Win-Win** revenue model where the platform only wins when the user wins:
* **Win-Win Model:** 10% fee of the total savings generated; no savings means no fee.
* **Pro Tier:** $49/month for unlimited contract monitoring, priority AI agents, and advanced clause alerts.
